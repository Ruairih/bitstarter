Avoid embarrassing mistakes that any native speaker of a language would never make. Open Translations matches fluent speakers of languages all over the world with business requiring simple, cost effective translation or proofreading services. Each document requiring proofing is submitted to two readers for extra thoroughness, their proofread submissions are then compared and the results mailed back to the sender.

Payments for work are performed seemlessly using Bitcoin. User wishing to provide translation services register with Open Translations, providing a bitcoin public address. Users wishing to employ our services send a bitcoin fee to our site public address which is then distributed to our translators.
